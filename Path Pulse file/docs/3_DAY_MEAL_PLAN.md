# Bio-Explorer 3-Day Fueling Protocol

For a Level 10 Scout on "Operation: Metabolism Spike." Balances BMI baseline with kinetic output.

---

## Day 1: Calibration Day (Low-Carb / High Protein)

**Goal:** Stabilize blood sugar, prep for fat-to-fuel conversion.

| Time | Station | Meal |
|------|---------|------|
| 08:00 | Fueling Station 1 | Two poached eggs over sautéed spinach and sliced avocado. |
| 13:00 | Fueling Station 2 | Grilled lemon-herb chicken breast, "Green Grid" salad (cucumber, kale, pumpkin seeds). |
| 16:00 | Kinetic Boost | Handful of raw almonds. |
| 19:00 | System Recovery | Baked salmon, roasted asparagus, cauliflower rice. |

**Protocol:** 2.5 L H₂O.

---

## Day 2: Expedition Day (Performance Carbs)

**Goal:** Fuel for a scheduled 5-mile Power Walk.

| Time | Station | Meal |
|------|---------|------|
| 08:00 | Fueling Station 1 | Steel-cut oats, blueberries, scoop of whey or plant protein. |
| 13:00 | Fueling Station 2 | Quinoa bowl with black beans, bell peppers, lean ground turkey. |
| 15:00 | Pre-Mission | One medium banana (quick glycogen). |
| 19:00 | System Recovery | Lean beef stir-fry with broccoli, snap peas, small portion brown rice. |

**Protocol:** 3.0 L H₂O (increased for mission recovery).

---

## Day 3: Reconstruction Day (Anti-Inflammatory)

**Goal:** Repair muscle, optimize BMI trendline.

| Time | Station | Meal |
|------|---------|------|
| 08:00 | Fueling Station 1 | Greek yogurt (plain), chia seeds, walnuts. |
| 13:00 | Fueling Station 2 | Tuna salad (Greek yogurt instead of mayo) in large romaine leaves. |
| 16:00 | Kinetic Boost | Celery sticks with a small amount of almond butter. |
| 19:00 | System Recovery | "Lab Special" — Roasted skinless chicken thigh, sweet potato, steamed zucchini. |

**Protocol:** 2.5 L H₂O. (Sweet potato magnesium aids Kinetic Recovery.)
